fix: replace all bare catch{} with catch(_e){} for esbuild/Vercel compatibility

esbuild on Vercel (node20 target) does not support optional catch binding (catch without
a variable). Replaced all instances of `} catch {` with `} catch (_e) {` throughout
server.ts to fix the build error:

  server.ts:4229:6: ERROR: Unexpected "catch"

Affected locations: fetchYahooQuote (x2), SSE stream heartbeat, hedgeFundData parse,
index data fallback, real price overlay, skip blocks, and all Supabase query catch blocks.
